# RSM — Just-DDJ Chain (v7.6-candidate, r4)

Convergence register. `::` denotes imperfect structural correspondence — not identity, translation, or equivalence. The DDJ is one independent compression of the same structural ontology, convergent with the framework, not its source and not derived from it. Machine-drafted (Claude, Fable 5); re-weighings are Will's domain.

Aligned to math r5 and physics r3. Rulings carried: **玄 :: Pₙ**; **玄牝 :: P₀**. Stratum tags: **[G]** attested in the Guodian bundles; **[R]** received-text only. Provenance tags per the parallax document: P1 / P2 / P3.

**New in r4 — two method rules, ruled this round:**

1. **Paleography is [G]-scoped.** Graph-level analysis (radicals, Chu forms as structural operators, loan identification) is licensed only where the graph is Guodian-attested. On [R] text the received transcription is taken as given and radicals are not decomposed. Consequence, applied below: readings that previously leaned on graph anatomy of [R] characters (名, 恆/常 in Ch. 1) are re-classed as structural proposals, not graph-evidence.
2. **The transcription layer is framework-blind.** Lexical glosses carry attested senses only; structural readings live in this chain, `::`-tagged, never in the gloss column. A gloss that already speaks RSM cannot refuse its author, and the P1 instruments depend on the layer being able to refuse. (Contaminated glosses identified and stripped this round: 生 "recurse," 玄 "Paradox," 民 "pierced eye / imposed construct," 萬 "modular brood.")

**Admissible-vocabulary constraint (Chu-engineer register), standing:** a reading of [G] material is admissible only if a person operating Warring States Chu technology — settling ponds, sluices, dikes, kilns, foundry bellows, silk-reeling, sickle harvest, rammed earth — could have meant it. The constraint cuts both ways: it forbids mystical importation *and* modern-physics importation equally. Tool kinematics are period-correct (sickle, not scythe: single-handed draw-arc; foundry box-bellows, not fireplace bellows; slow wheel). Guard on the guard: "a Chu engineer would have seen X" is itself a pattern-completion channel and gets flagged like any other projection.

## 天地不仁; the bellows; 守中 **[mixed strata: the Guodian Ch. 5 fragment (A23) carries only the bellows lines 猶橐籥與／虛而不屈／動而愈出; 天地不仁 and 守中 are received-only]**

**天地不仁 :: no inherent preferred orientation — the isotropy premise, nothing more.** **[R][P2]** The line asserts the symmetry; it does not perform the lift. What the derivation does with the premise (S¹ → S² → ℝ³, the substrate section of the math chain) belongs to the math and is not credited to the text. This supersedes r3's "do not route through 常":

- **常 / implicit register:** the premise holds unbroken — global symmetry [SO(3)]; the math lifts the orbit S¹ → S²; three dimensions necessary (uniqueness open).
- **可 / parametric register** (e.g., life on earth): the symmetry is broken by co-aligned physical gradients — gravity and sunlight orienting one vertical [SO(2)] — and the sphere of Pₙ-equivalent loci reduces to a ring about the privileged axis.
- **可/常 :: symmetry-broken / symmetry-intact :: S¹ / S².** The old caveat's guard survives as term-distinctness: 不仁 = "no preference"; 常 = "nothing to prefer with"; not fused. The gradient co-alignment that gives 可-register life a single clean vertical is contingent physical fact — the parametric register wearing its parameter openly.

*Ruled this round (premise-only reading; caveat rewrite). The ring/sphere geometry is verified in the derivation and physics registers; the DDJ supplies the premise a name, nothing more.*

**The bellows — the strongest [G] mechanical anchor.** 猶橐籥與／虛而不屈／動而愈出: under the Chu-engineer register this is the foundry box-bellows, a **bounded reciprocating oscillator whose poles are failure states, not goals**. Drive either mode to its pole and hold it — plunger locked down, or drawn past the seal — and the tool stops being a bellows; the two operators must alternate, and neither stroke-limit is occupiable as a resting state. 虛而不屈 :: the void-mode never driven to collapse — carrying capacity persists; 動而愈出 :: sustained output *because* the system never seals.

- **Register discipline:** the stroke limits are **0ₙ and 1ₙ** — operational limit-positions within the frame — not |0|/|1|. Only the ground trades in the absolute faces. (Correction against an imported draft that read the poles as absolutes.) The lung carries the same fact physically: residual volume — the lung structurally cannot expel its last air — a clean independent instance of 0ₙ ≠ |0|. *[P2; illustration, not derivation]*
- **The projection bridge, held at its weight:** uniform circular motion viewed edge-on is simple harmonic oscillation, so the 1D stroke is what the orbit about Oₙ looks like from inside a single mode-axis. Honest as an intuition pump; not upgraded — not every oscillation is a projected rotation, and a real stroke is not a pure sinusoid. *[P2; do not promote]*
- 弗居 (成而弗居, A17) remains the DDJ's dedicated term for constitutive impossibility — completes yet structurally-cannot-dwell — and names the aperture; the bellows gives it a workshop instance: no stroke-pole is dwellable. 弗 (structural impossibility) ≠ 不 (contingent negation), a Guodian distinction the received text flattens. **[G]**

## Water in Bundle A **[new section; [G] anchors only]**

Stratum honesty first: the famous water chapters are received-only — Ch. 8 (上善若水) and Ch. 78 (water overcoming the hard) are **not in Guodian**. The [G] water corpus is six anchors: the 浴-for-谷 orthography (the valley-graph carries the water radical in the strip itself — 百浴, 小浴; graphic, not interpretive); Ch. 66 (江海 as lord of the hundred valleys **by lying below them**); Ch. 32 (the Dao in the world as valley-streams to river and sea); Ch. 15 (濁而靜之徐清 — muddy water, stilled, gradually clears; and 涉 — the winter river-crossing); and 溺 at A37.

**Candidate invariant:** water :: traversal at zero misalignment cost — takes the lowest position, descends without exertion (溺, sinking-under as the Dao's functioning, 道之用), converges without command (江海／百浴下), clarifies by stilling (徐清). The 無為 limit of traversal; the DDJ leg of the energy-cost function stated in the physics chain. *[P2; candidate; Will's re-weighing]*

Discipline notes binding this section:

- **溺 is descriptive, not pathological.** The received 弱 moralizes what the bamboo keeps descriptive; any water reading that treats sinking as system-failure commits against 溺 the same error the chain convicts the received text of. (An imported draft did exactly this; corrected.)
- **Stilling is clarification, not death.** 濁而靜之徐清 is an operator's instruction — Chu hydraulic practice used settling basins deliberately; the pond-keeper stills water *on purpose*. Settling-as-collapse readings invert the attested line.
- **Phonophores are not semantics.** 工 in 江 and 每/母 in 海 are phonetic; "water doing work" and "Mother Water" are folk etymology and carry no structural load. Struck.
- **海 is a gradient position, not the substrate.** The attested sea-image is *lowest position — convergence without command*: a limit-position within the gradient structure. The substrate is parameter-free; different objects. 海 :: 恆 is refused.
- **浧 — open.** The sheet glosses 浧 as mud/settle; standard scholarship reads it as the loan for 盈 (fill), which Ch. 9's syntax (持而浧之) favors. If the mud sense is ruled to hold against the loan reading, that is a logged divergence from consensus; until ruled, no reading built on 浧-as-settling carries weight. *[open; Will's to rule]*
