## Foundational conditional

If reality is infinite, it has no terminal resolution: no minimum scale, no maximum extent. The chain derives what follows if this holds. Premises used beyond the conditional are named at first use: Postulates Q and F; the Frame-Universality principle (FU); Definition T; the seating commitment.

## Excluded terminal condition

P₀: absolute indistinguishability. What is not distinguishable does not obtain. |0| and |1| are the same incoherence; neither obtains.

## The ground: because P₀ cannot hold, O₁

The conditional's antecedent carries existential import: reality obtains, and is infinite. What obtains is distinguishable. With P₀ incoherent on both faces, at least one distinction obtains — the first distinction, the first origin.

**Because P₀ is logically incoherent, O₁.** The exclusion is generative, not merely restrictive. Identity through opposition: no prior state, no parent frame — not parturition. *[derived within the conditional]*

**The generative proportion:**

**Pₙ : O₍ₙ₊₁₎ :: P₍ₙ₋₁₎ : Oₙ :: P₀ : O₁**

The ratio — an origin obtaining from a term that cannot hold — is invariant down to the ground; the mechanism is not (parturition for n ≥ 1; identity through opposition at the ground). Constant ratio, distinguished mechanism. The generative line is the alternating sequence P₀, O₁, P₁, O₂, … in which each (Pₙ, O₍ₙ₊₁₎) pair for n ≥ 1 is one locus under two frame-readings — parent: cannot persist; child: obtains as origin — and the first pair alone has no locus on its P-side.

## Form and calibration: Frame-Universality

**Family invariance: the calibration is selection-free.** Apply FU uniformly across the two-parameter family of conserved-product structures:

- Primary family {XY = +μ1ₙ}: seats at (±√(μ1ₙ), 0); FU gives Q_t = α·μ1ₙ = μ1ₙ, so **α = 1 for every μ**.
- Conjugate family {XY = −μ1ₙ}: seats at (0, ±√(μ1ₙ)); FU gives γ·μ1ₙ = μ1ₙ, so **γ = 1 for every μ**.
