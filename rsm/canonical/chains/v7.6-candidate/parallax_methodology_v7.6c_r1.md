## The `::` discipline

`::` denotes imperfect structural correspondence: two expressions from different registers point at the same structural fact; neither captures it fully; each carries register-specific distortion. It forbids substitution, upgrade to identity, and congruence by default. A chain A :: B :: C is not pairwise overlap; it is the claim that all terms converge on one named invariant. If the invariant cannot be named, the chain breaks into separate claims. Each through-line names the invariant first, then each register's pointing.

## What convergence does and does not establish

**Establishes:** the same structural shape recurs across registers compressed independently — a derivation, an ancient text, and a body of physical law not built from one another.

**Does not establish:** that reality satisfies the conditional (held humble); nor that convergence among registers assembled by one author, assisted by one class of tools, substitutes for external verification. The load-bearing independent element is the **foundational conditional** — it predates the framework and survives removal of any chain. The derivation stands or falls on the conditional, not the correspondences.

## The independence audit

"Independent registers" flattens a distinction the method needs. Each register mixes an independent component with an authored one; evidence lives only in the independent part.

- **DDJ:** the *received text* is independent — ancient, fixed, not authored here, and via the Guodian strips physically anterior by ~2,300 years. The *readings* are authored, LLM-assisted, framework-aware. The text testifies only where it *resists* a wanted reading, or where a reading was committed before the formal result existed. **Stratum caveat:** the ground-level anchors (玄牝, 道生一, the Ch. 1 cascade) are received-text-only; the engine-level anchors (反, 相生, 道法自然) are Guodian-attested. The newest and deepest correspondences are the least anciently witnessed.
- **Physics:** the *facts* are independent (λf = c; the uncertainty floor; Sp(2,ℝ) ≅ SU(1,1); GR's manifold excision of the singularity). The *selection* is not — a corpus was searched and misses go unlogged unless surfaced. The register's honest instruments are its logged misses (Boyle over the ideal gas law) and predictions sharp enough to fail.
- **Math:** the *proofs* are independent in the strongest sense available — they check under symbolic verification with no access to intent. The *choice of what to formalize* is not. The math register's unique value is that it is the only one that has repeatedly said *no* to its own author.

In one line: **the text is independent but the reading isn't; the facts are independent but the selection isn't; the proofs are independent but the agenda isn't.**

## Provenance tagging (a third CAVP axis)

Every correspondence carries, alongside accuracy and precision, a provenance tag:

- **P1 — predicted:** committed before the formal result or before the passage was consulted. (Rare; the controls below manufacture them.)
- **P2 — found:** identified after the formal result existed. (The current default: the oscillator instance, the manifold-excision fit, the sum/difference invariants.)
- **P3 — imported:** the register was adjusted to match another or the shared model. (The parturition notation, honestly.)

P1 carries weight against the shared-authorship objection; P2 carries reach; P3 carries coherence only. Over versions, the P1:P2:P3 ratio becomes the framework's measured baseline — the thing the parallax analogy has been missing.

## Five controls (measuring the unknown baseline)

1. **Control corpus.** Run the identical `::` procedure, identical effort, against a comparably compressed ancient text not used in construction (Heraclitus, the Xici, Parmenides, Nāgārjuna). Low correspondence density → the method discriminates and the DDJ convergence means something; comparable density everywhere → the method has been measuring its own generosity. The single most powerful available test; win-win (a genuinely independent second text at high density would itself be a fourth register).
2. **Permutation control.** Run the reading against a chapter-shuffled DDJ; correspondences surviving permutation were never about the text's structure.
3. **Preregistered readings.** Commit, versioned, which DDJ material should carry the next math result before formalizing. Postdicted fits are P2; predicted fits are P1. The Ch. 40 strip-variant sorting is a ready first case.
4. **Blind reformalization.** Hand the conditional plus the gradient premise — nothing else — to an independent mathematician or a contextless model; convergence is baseline-independent, divergence localizes which steps are forced. (The symbolic re-verification in these sessions is a weak version.)
5. **One sharp prediction.** Operationalize a falsifiable prediction to the point a measurement could embarrass it. Power-law nesting (physics prediction 4) is the most operationalizable; define observables and the number that would falsify it.
