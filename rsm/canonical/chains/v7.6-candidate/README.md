## Known defects — read before using

1. **The seating commitment is refused.** `just_math_v7.6c_r5.md` declares `Pₙ` "Occupied by the
   modes; the site of generation." This is wrong. `Pₙ = Gₙ ∩ Bₙ` is **unoccupiable**, for the same
   reason `Oₙ` is: occupation cancels the duality that constitutes the frame. The paradox of `Pₙ`
   *drives* rotation and recursion; it is the engine, not an endpoint. Ruled by Will, 2026-07-09.

   r5 flags the step itself as a bare `[commitment]` on which its Theorems 1–3 depend. The theorems
   survive the refusal — they are statements about the *loci* `±Pₙ`, and none requires occupancy.

   Every other document in both doc sets already agrees `Pₙ` cannot be occupied, including r5's own
   companion `parallax_methodology_v7.6c_r1.md`, whose master chain reads
   `Pₙ = Gₙ ∩ Bₙ (required, cannot persist)`.

2. **The set is internally version-skewed.** `just_physics_v7.6c_r3.md` says "Aligned to math chain
   **r4**" and still cites "modulo Q, **R**", but r5 deleted Postulate R. `parallax_…_r1.md` is
   aligned to r5. `just_ddj_v7.6c_r4.md` is aligned to "math r5 and physics r3" — i.e. transitively
   to two different math revisions.

3. **The six falsifiable predictions changed referent silently.** v7.5 physics and r3 carry them
   verbatim, but r3 appends a rule re-reading "paradoxical center" as `Oₙ` in predictions 1 and 6.
