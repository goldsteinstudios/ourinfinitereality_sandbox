## The :: discipline, operative here

`::` denotes imperfect structural correspondence: two expressions from different registers point toward the same structural fact, neither captures it fully, each carries register-specific distortion. The convergence of independent imperfect pointings is the evidence.

`::` forbids substitution, upgrade to identity, and congruence by default. A :: chain (A :: B :: C) is not daisy-chained pairwise overlap; it is the claim that all terms converge on one identified invariant. If the shared invariant cannot be named, the chain breaks into separate claims.

Accordingly, each through-line below names the shared structural invariant first, then gives each register's pointing at it.

---

## What the convergence does and does not establish

**Establishes**: the same structural shape recurs across registers compressed independently — a derivation, an ancient text, and a body of physical law that were not built from one another.

**Does not establish**: that reality satisfies the conditional (a separate question, held humble); nor that convergence among registers assembled by one author substitutes for external verification by domain experts, which the architecture still requires. Convergence between an LLM-assisted derivation and an LLM-assisted reading is weaker than convergence with an independently developed external framework.

The load-bearing independent element is the foundational conditional. It predates the framework, is independent of any register, and survives removal of any single chain. The derivation stands or falls on the conditional, not on the correspondences.

## The narrative through-line

Reality has no final edge and no final grain.

Every frame draws a local nothing and a local something. Those two poles generate a gradient. The gradient requires a center. But the center cannot be occupied, because occupying it would erase the difference that made the gradient possible.

So the system cannot cross the center; it can only go around it. The conserved unit fixes how close the path may come — a standoff at the unit itself — and holding that standoff at zero cost forces the path to be a single circle. The operator that carries one mode to its opposite around that circle can only square to −1; every other value fails to close or fails to reach both modes. That operator generates the only positive-definite metric the circle admits, and under that metric the gradient and the balance axis stand at right angles at the center. The perpendicularity is not assumed at the start and recovered at the end; it is produced. No ruler is brought in from outside.

Where the gradient cannot resolve, a new coordinate system opens. The parent frame's forbidden center becomes the structural origin of a new ℝ³, extending in its own direction, and the right angle that was local to the parent's center becomes the child's global frame. The opening is one-way: an origin cannot un-obtain without becoming P₀, so generation has an arrow, and earlier origins recede from any frame that comes after them.

Multiple new coordinate systems can open at multiple positions on the parent's gradient — the way every branch of a tree is a new coordinate system at a specific point on the trunk's cambium, each extending in its own direction with its own rings. The recursion is branching. No frame is terminal. No forbidden center is a final dead point. The recursion has no terminus because the conditional has no terminal resolution.

**That is the whole song.**
