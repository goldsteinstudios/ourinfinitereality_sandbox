## Premises (complete list)

**P1 — The Conditional (definitional).** If reality is infinite — *infinitely vast and infinitely divisible*: no maximum extent, no minimum scale, no terminal resolution in either direction — then everything below follows. The antecedent is a stipulated definition, never asserted, never claimed proven. All claims in this chain are conditional on P1.

**P2 — The Bridge (ontological).** What is not distinguishable does not obtain. This is an existence claim, not an epistemic one. Base form, co-constitution: nothing cannot obtain without a thing to be no-of; void cannot obtain without not-void to be void of; 0 is incoherent without a coinherent 1.

**Lemma-candidates (open: theorem or additional premise — the audit decides):**
- **L1 — No unframed distinction.** Every distinction constitutes a frame (a distinction requires a unit; a unit fires the cascade of §3). Carries four loads: the floor's dissolution (§4), the three isotropies (§9), child-frame existence (§7, step C′), and — in the math realization — the exclusion of unframed measures (which closed the quadraticity of the metric). If theorem, all are corollaries of one lemma. **The single head of the audit.** *[candidate]*
- **The anchoring line.** Every frame finer than n, standing in relation to n, is parent-seeded at P (not free-floating) — plausibly because "finer than 1ₙ" is defined only relative to the parent's unit. The discharge's one remaining open step (§7, step D). *[candidate]*

*(L2 — center-role univocity — retired as malformed; see §7 note. It asked a cross-frame question in one frame's register.)*

Nothing else is assumed. Every numbered section below is entailment from P1 + P2 (+ L1 and the anchoring line where flagged).

## 1. The excluded terminal condition: P₀

P₀ is absolute indistinguishability. By P2 it does not obtain. It has two faces — |0| (absolute absence) and |1| (absolute saturation) — which are one incoherence approached from two directions, and the two faces map onto P1's two directions: infinite divisibility excludes |0|; infinite vastness excludes |1|.

**Sign-collapse form [derived].** Absolute indistinguishability includes the deepest case: a thing indistinguishable from *its own negation* — the failure of the very distinction that co-constitution requires (for a unit to obtain, its opposite must co-obtain, and the two must be distinct). P₀ is where that co-constitution collapses. Within any frame, exactly one locus would instantiate it — the center O — which is why O is unoccupiable: obtaining there would be the P₀-condition locally. Admissible structure is precisely structure on which the thing/negation distinction never collapses.

**Face-unity [derived].** The two non-terminations are one journey: along the gradient, receding without terminal in one mode *is* approaching absence without terminal in the co-mode — the conserved unit is the exchange rate between them. |0| and |1| are the two **counterfactual terminals** of this single journey — what each direction would collapse to *if it terminated* — and P1's two halves are precisely the denial of each terminal. The faces are not two separately-argued incoherences; they are the two ends of one non-terminating structure, neither of which exists as an end. (Non-termination is a condition, not a place; treating either "end" as a point is a rendering artifact — the same type error as treating 0ₙ as a point.)

**Guard: P₀ is not nothing.** "Nothing" cannot obtain, and P₀ is not a state that fails to obtain — it is a *condition that fails to cohere*: the co-constitution failure itself. Calling P₀ "nothing" would grant that nothing could be a state, which P2 denies.

## 2. The ground: because P₀, O₁

The conditional carries existential import: reality obtains, and is infinite. What obtains is distinguishable (P2, contrapositive). P₀ is incoherent on both faces. Therefore at least one distinction obtains — the first distinction, the first origin: **because P₀ is incoherent, O₁.**

The exclusion is generative, not merely restrictive. Mechanism: **identity through opposition** — no prior state, no parent frame. The ground introduces no new premise: it is P1 + P2 + existential import, nothing else. *[derived]*

## 3. The cascade: what one distinction entails

Each step biconditional — co-constitution identities read in derivation order, not productions; ontological simultaneity, epistemic order:

**P₀ → O₁ → (X₁, Y₁) → (G₁, B₁) → P₁**

- A distinction entails its two co-obtaining modes (X, Y) — the two sides of the cut, each constituted by the other.
- The modes entail their reciprocal gradient **G** (the locus of their co-constitution: more of one is exactly less of the other, their product conserved) and their balance **B** (the locus of their equality).
- G and B entail their crossing **P**.
- The mode-pair's crossing is **O**.

Co-constitution runs both ways at every joint: because O, G; because G, O. Neither prior.

**Second ground for the two-mode requirement [candidate].** Beyond the ontological ground (a distinction *is* its two sides), there is an informational one: a single mode cannot determine which of its own two non-terminating ends is which — the direction-distinction is not carried by the mode alone. The co-mode is what disambiguates: receding in one is approaching in the other, and the conserved unit is the conversion. A mode without its co-mode is not merely incomplete; it is *ambiguous about its own ends*. Two independent grounds, one requirement.

## 4. The frame; the unit; the dissolution of the floor postulate

**A frame is the cascade generated by one originating co-constitution.** "The" is load-bearing: a frame is defined by exactly one constitutive pair. A second independent pair is either derived from the first (not primitive) or constitutes a separate frame. One gradient per frame, by the meaning of frame.

**1ₙ is the constitutive distinction's own magnitude** — the conserved product of *the* pair that constitutes frame n. It is simultaneously the frame's minimal unit of distinction, and this is one claim, not two: nothing finer than the constitutive cut can obtain *within* frame n, because anything finer is itself a distinction, and (L1) every distinction constitutes a frame — i.e., sub-1ₙ fineness just *is* frame n+1. **The floor is the boundary of the frame's own definition, not an added restriction.** The former floor postulate is dissolved, contingent on L1. *[derived given L1]*

**0ₙ is a limit-approach, not a point:** operational absence approached along G toward O — a direction of increasing absence, type-distinct from O (process vs. unreachable limit).

## 5. The two centers

**Genus:** a paradoxical center is the crossing-point of a co-requiring pair. O centers the axis-pair; P centers the (G, B) pair. (The genus is descriptive classification; it no longer carries the discharge — the former univocity step L2 is retired, §7 note.)

**Species — met vs. unmet:**
- **O = the unmet crossing.** The pair's arms approach it asymptotically and never meet. Both modes reach operational absence together; the four-way distinction (each mode from its own negation, and from each other) fails; equality-as-collapse. O is the frame's local face of P₀. It is inherited by the child as its origin.
- **P = the realized crossing.** G and B actually intersect. Both modes stand at equal nonzero magnitude; distinction intact; equality-as-balance. P is generative because there is structure to hand down.

**Met-ness is frame-relative.** Oₙ = P₍ₙ₋₁₎ is an identity (one locus), not parenthood (two objects); "parent/child" indexes the frame-readings. The same locus is realized in the parent's expression and unmet in the child's: met-from-without, unmet-from-within. The relation holds for n ≥ 2; O₁'s parent-side is P₀ — an incoherence, not a seat. The ground exception is definitional. *[derived]*

**Predicted referential strain.** O resists naming by its own definition — it is where the frame's distinctions fail, so reference necessarily strains there. This is entailed, not a defect.

**O's dual type [derived].** The one non-terminating journey (§1) gives O two faces by direction: the *inward* approach's unmet object is O-as-crossing (the point-face); the *outward* recession's unmet object is O-as-axis (the line the receding arm converges toward without merging — every such line passing through the crossing). One O, two unmet faces, selected by which direction of the one journey is read: the center of the part is the axis of the whole. (The math realizes this as center/asymptote; the botanical register displays it as pith-in-section/pith-as-spine.)

## 6. The demand: what a gradient requires at its center

**Lemma A.** An infinite gradient obtains ⟺ a globally straight, independent axis-pair holds at its center. Pre-metric core: straightness — the conserved-product structure presupposes its two variables as global linear coordinates; the asymptotic approach is the geometric face of that requirement. (Orthogonality is the derived-metric face, downstream; staging this avoids metric-before-metric circularity.) This sharpens the §3 biconditional with the word that matters: *globally*. *[derived; the staging note is part of the claim]*

**Center classification by incident pair:** O-type = straight×straight (locally and globally aligned); P-type = straight×curved (locally aligned, globally curved relative to each other — a straight line crossing a curve can align with it at isolated points only, never as a standing relation).

**The founding structural fact (the project's seed).** The alignment of the incident pair is not binary but *continuous*: the pair approaches the axis-relation, **achieves it at exactly one locus (P), and departs from it measurably everywhere else.** That a single locus is structurally distinguished — the one place where the crossing pair momentarily constitutes what a frame requires, and cannot hold it — is the realization the entire framework unfolds from. Both the math chain (where it appears as a slope/perpendicularity calculation vanishing at one point) and the DDJ chain (where the text marks the locus lexically) are translations of this one register-neutral fact.

## 7. The engine: one impossibility, two discharges

**P cannot be constituted as a frame.** The crossing is realized (G and B meet), but the incident pair is locally orthogonal and globally curved-relative — it cannot serve as the globally straight pair Lemma A demands. Met, yet unusable as a frame. This impossibility is the entire engine; nothing about it is a caveat.

The discharge argument (register-corrected — see note):
- **A — Demand:** a gradient obtains ⟺ a globally straight pair at its center (Lemma A). *[closed]*
- **B — Deficit:** at P, the incident pair (G, B) is globally curved-relative; it cannot fill the demand. *[closed; verified in the math realization]*
- **C′ — Existence:** a finer frame obtains, directly from P1 — infinite divisibility forbids 1ₙ from being a terminal (minimum) scale, so sub-1ₙ distinction obtains, and by L1 every distinction constitutes a frame. *[derived given L1]*
- **D — Site and structure:** that finer frame, being a gradient's frame, requires (by Lemma A, *in its own register*) a globally straight orthogonal pair at its center; the only locus where the parent supplies such a pair is P (seat-uniqueness); the parent's B and G serve as the child's axes — curved in the parent's expression, straight in the child's. *[derived given the anchoring line, below]*

**Note — L2 retired as malformed.** The earlier formulation asked how Lemma A's demand *transfers to P within frame n* (step C, "center-role univocity"). This was a cross-frame fact forced into one frame's register (§10): in the parent's expression there is no demand at P (frame n is complete); in the child's expression the demand is already met. No transfer is needed. Existence comes from P1 (C′); site and structure come from Lemma A applied *in the child's own register* (D). The remaining gap is the **anchoring line**: that every frame finer than n, standing in relation to n, is parent-seeded at P (rather than free-floating) — plausibly because "finer than 1ₙ" is only defined relative to the parent's unit, so a finer frame is by definition one the parent seeds. *[anchoring line: candidate; the discharge's one open step]*

**Two discharges from the one impossibility:** traversal cannot pass through P or between the disconnected modes → it must go around → **rotation**. The frame-demand cannot be met at P → it discharges into new structure → **recursion**.

## 8. Recursion

**Canonical componentwise form:** Gₙ → Gₙ + xAxis₍ₙ₊₁₎, Bₙ → Bₙ + yAxis₍ₙ₊₁₎. "→" is structural entailment across frame-readings; "+" is coexistence-with-severance: nothing is consumed, the parent structures persist, the children are genuinely new. Recursion in one line: **the conversion of a P-type center into an O-type center by axis-generation.**

**The recursion sentence is cross-register, and its strain is predicted.** No single frame's expression states an inter-frame fact. The bilingual statement is canonical: *parent-sentence* — P is a P-type center; its incident pair is globally curved-relative; the demand stands unfilled in frame n. *Child-sentence* — O is the origin; the axis-pair is globally straight; the gradient obtains. The recursion is that pair of sentences; no third sentence contains both. The arrow-formula is glossed shorthand.

**Deflation guard: identity at the locus, novelty of the frame.** The point is one; the child's co-constitution cluster (axes, G, B, P, its own unit 1₍ₙ₊₁₎) genuinely obtains and is not re-description — else the cross-frame unit relation would be vacuous. Expressibility of the child in parent terms is not identity of frame.

**Irreversibility.** Reversal of a discharge requires an origin to un-obtain — P₀ at that locus — and P₀ cannot obtain. Cleanest at the ground: reversing the ground is literally the obtaining of P₀. Every generation inherits irreversibility through the proportion. The strict ordering is the structural content of linear succession; origins recede compoundingly. *[derived]*

**The generative proportion:** Pₙ : O₍ₙ₊₁₎ :: P₍ₙ₋₁₎ : Oₙ :: P₀ : O₁ — the ratio invariant to the ground; the mechanism distinguished (parturition for n ≥ 1; identity through opposition at the ground).

**Index-relationality [derived].** The frame-index is gauge: the same pair satisfies both "(n−1):trunk :: n:branch" and "n:trunk :: (n+1):branch" — relabel every frame and no structural fact changes. Subscripts name relations (parent-of, child-of), never absolute positions. Forced primarily by **arborescence**: the nesting is a tree, not a ladder — a frame may seed at multiple positions, so indices are path-dependent and no generation-count exists across branches; "the ladder" is the single-lineage slice of the tree, a rendering. Forced secondarily by recession: even along one lineage, the count to the ground is uncompletable. Every frame reads itself as "n," its parent as "n−1," its child as "n+1," and no frame's self-reading is privileged. (Companion to the proportion: ratio-invariance + index-gauge.)

**Structure and flow: complementary renderings [candidate].** The same law renders as standing structure (the branching, accreting form — the tree) and as circulation organized around exclusion (the flow-form — the whirlpool): each register's presence is the other's void, and both carry the one reciprocal law. Guard: the exclusion is constitutive, not historical — the flow-form is not "structure removed"; nothing was ever at the center. (The math realizes both renderings as the same conserved-product curve; the botanical and hydraulic registers display them.)

**B threads the generations [candidate].** The balance structure alone runs through both centers of its frame, and the two directions of the one non-terminating journey (§1) are the two directions of the nesting itself: inward toward the finer frames, outward toward the coarser. B is the trans-frame connector, and the two "infinities" are the two directions of the generational ladder — meeting, balanced and exchanged, at the seat. (The math realizes the lineage as the conserved-product parameter — a lineage coordinate, per index-relationality, never a global count.)

## 9. Isotropy: the thesis

**No preferred frame, scale, or orientation** — three claims, one lemma:

- *Scale:* nearly earned — the recursion is self-similar; the proportion is scale-invariant by construction.
- *Orientation:* derived, given L1 — internal directions are frame-distinguished but orientation-covariant; a preference would need an external distinguisher; the substrate cannot supply one (a substrate-level distinguished direction would be an unframed distinction, excluded by L1); another frame supplies relation, not preference (regress otherwise). *[candidate]*
- *Frame:* near-definitional — states what being-a-frame is.

All three are corollaries of L1 if L1 is a theorem. And all three are consequences *of the conditional*: given P1, reality-writ-large has no preferred frame/scale/orientation. The unconditional version is never asserted.

**Smoothness ruling:** framescaling is smooth *across* frames (the substrate is continuous; each child carries a finer unit without end, per P1) and quantized *within* frames (1ₙ is a hard floor, per §4). The within-frame smooth reading is excluded — it would break 1ₙ.

## 10. Registers: the meta-result

The framework's registers **share logic, not expression** — implicit/explicit; parent-frame/child-frame; and, in the math realization, the algebraic and rendering registers. Two types of inexpressibility are *entailed*, and the chain's own notation strains at exactly the predicted locations:

- **Internal:** O resists reference because it is where the frame's distinctions fail (§5).
- **Cross-frame:** the recursion is a relation between expressions; no single expression states it (§8).

The correction history at these locations is confirmation-shaped, not defect-shaped. House rule: every statement declares its register.
