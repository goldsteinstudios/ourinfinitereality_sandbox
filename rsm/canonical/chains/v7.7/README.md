## Premises (from the structural chain)

- **P1 — The Conditional** (definitional): reality is infinite — infinitely vast *and* infinitely
  divisible; no terminal resolution in either direction. Stipulated, never claimed proven.
- **P2 — The Bridge** (ontological): what is not distinguishable does not obtain.
- **L1 — No unframed distinction** *(lemma-candidate under audit)*: every distinction constitutes a
  frame. Carries four loads (floor-dissolution, the three isotropies, child-frame existence, the
  exclusion of unframed measures). **The single head of the audit.**
- **The anchoring line** *(candidate)*: every finer frame is parent-seeded at P. The discharge's one
  remaining open step.

Math chain's working premise set: **Conditional + Bridge + Definition T + the seating commitment + L1.**

- **Oₙ** = the **unmet** crossing (axis-pair). Arms approach asymptotically, never meet; the frame's
