Correspondence register. `::` denotes imperfect structural correspondence — not identity, translation-equivalence, or congruence. Aligned to RSM chain v7.7 and math chain v7.7. Machine-drafted (Claude, Fable 5); re-weighings are Will's domain. Stratum tags: **[G]** attested in the Guodian bundles; **[R]** received-text only.

**Evidential posture (repriced, v7.7).** The DDJ is generatively upstream of the RSM — Will built the framework through the text. Therefore: (i) the founding convergence was real and was spent in the discovery context (it bought attention; that update was rational); (ii) present-state convergence between this chain and the derivation carries **no public evidential weight** (agreement with one's own seed is expected); (iii) what survives publicly is *compression quality* — 2,500 years of transmission pressure is evidence the text is dense and load-bearing, which grounds the instrument below, not the thesis. The DDJ is re-typed from witness to **subject of translation**: this chain's success criterion is translation-fidelity (philological), independent of the math chain's criterion (derivational necessity). Neither claim borrows the other's evidence.

**The instrument (checksum doctrine).** The DDJ and the mathematical notation are two compressions with non-overlapping failure modes; each audits the other. Four flows, two legitimate, two forbidden:
- **Text → math, notation layer (legitimate):** where the text has ≥2 lexical items and the math one symbol, flag a candidate hidden distinction; *the math must then verify or refute it with its own resources.* Question-import, never answer-import.
- **Math → commentary, reading layer (legitimate):** given rival glosses of a line, structural coherence discriminates; the math checksums the commentary tradition, not the strips.
- **Text → math, premise layer (forbidden):** no character enters a derivation. The retired math term "aperture" (gate-vocabulary, 門-bleed) is the standing example.
- **Math → text, attestation layer (forbidden):** no structure overrides what the strips say.
