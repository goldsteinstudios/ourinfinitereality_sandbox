## The Conditional

**If reality is truly infinite** — no terminal resolution in either direction, no smallest unit and no largest unit — **then what obtains must be distinguishable. What is not distinguishable does not obtain.**

This is a conditional, not an assertion. The framework does not claim reality is truly infinite. It derives what follows if it is. If the conditional is rejected, the derivation does not apply. No additional premises are introduced at any point.

**Why "truly infinite" over "infinitely divisible":** The conditional is symmetric. Infinite divisibility (no floor) and infinite vastness (no ceiling) are two directions of the same structural condition. The symmetric treatment of |0| (absence-direction) and |1| (saturation-direction) as the same structural incoherence — P₀ — follows directly from this symmetric conditional. It does not require a separate argument.

## The ⽞ / 牝 / ⽞牝 / ⽣ / 相⽣ Cluster

Four distinguishable structural functions in a structural grammar — not parallel concepts. Mathematical notation flattens them; the DDJ preserves the distinctions.

- **⽞** — the paradoxical condition; must exist, cannot persist; constitutive of every Pₙ
- **牝** — generative capacity; the structural property of a locus to produce; the capacity, not the action

## The :: Operator Discipline

`::` denotes imperfect structural correspondence between framework-specific distinctions.

**What :: asserts:** Two expressions from different frameworks point toward the same structural fact. Neither captures it fully. Both carry distortion specific to their framework. The convergence of independent, imperfect pointings is the evidence.

**What :: does not assert:** Identity. Translation. Equivalence.

**What :: forbids:**

1. **No substitution** — from A :: B, you may not replace A with B in any expression
2. **No upgrade** — A :: B does not entail A ≡ B or A = B
3. **No congruence by default** — from A :: B, you may not infer C(A) :: C(B) for arbitrary C; each side's framework-specific distortion propagates under embedding

**What :: chains require:** A :: B :: C is not daisy-chained pairwise overlap. It is the claim that all terms converge on the same structural invariant — the undrawn body between all the strokes. If the shared invariant cannot be identified, break the chain into separate :: claims.

**Verbal discipline:** In prose without :: notation, use "to me, that looks just like…" rather than "X is Y." The verbal form preserves the imperfect-correspondence character.

**RSM vocabulary is not coating.** :: correspondences must be earned through the framework's own logic. Applying RSM vocabulary to material that has not been derived through the structural chain is the principal AI failure mode.
