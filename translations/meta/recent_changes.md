### Framework Synthesis — Major Additions

**File:** `translations/meta/framework_synthesis_2025-12-07.md`

New sections added:

1. **The Core Formula** — Explicit notation for recursion:
   - `Gₙ ∩ Bₙ = Pₙ` (gradient intersects boundary at paradox point)
   - `Pₙ → Oₙ₊₁` (paradox becomes next origin)

2. **The Hyperboloid Shape** — Mathematical form of the gradient-boundary relationship:
   - 2D: `xy = 1`
   - 3D: `x² + z² = 1/y²`
   - Valley point P at (1,1) = 谷

3. **Two Directions of Approach to the Origin** — Tathata/ziran mapping:
   - Compression (tathata): toward specificity, suchness
   - Expansion (ziran): toward relationality, self-so-ness
   - Same center, different directions, same unreachability

4. **Cross-Traditional Mapping Table**:
   | DDJ | Sanskrit | Physics | Geometric Operation |
   |-----|----------|---------|---------------------|
   | 妙 | tathata | wave | Compression toward O |
   | 徼 | svabhava | particle | Expansion from O |
   | 玄 | sunyata | superposition | Origin O |
   | 谷 | madhyama | ground state | Paradox point P |

5. **The Plug Problem** — Why self-reference is forced:
   - No outside to infinite reality
   - Ruler can't measure the concept of length
   - Forces recursion (自然)

6. **The Four Impossibilities** — Why the origin cannot be reached:
   | Level | What Fails | DDJ Reference |
   |-------|------------|---------------|
   | Physical | Instruments | 夷 |
   | Intellectual | Concepts | 希 |
   | Epistemological | Knowledge | 微 |
   | Ontological | Being | 玄 |
