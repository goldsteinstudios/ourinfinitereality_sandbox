## Key Structural Insights

### [Wei Wu Wei vs Wu Wei](./meta/wei_wu_wei_vs_wu_wei_2025-12-08.md)
為無為 (wéi wú wéi) as "act-rest-act"—the complete oscillation cycle, not mystical paradox. Why the bellows must compress AND release. Why the scythe must swing AND step. The difference between 無為 (wú wéi, arc-action) and 為 (wéi, linear force).

### [Governance as Hydraulics](./meta/governance_hydraulic_etymology_2025-12-08.md)
治 (zhì) = 氵 (shuǐ, water) + 台 (tái, platform). The aqueduct cross-section encoded in the character. Why 治水 (zhì shuǐ, water management) came before 治國 (zhì guó, state governance). Governance as building channels that let gravity work.

### [The Scythe, Not the Knife](./meta/scythe_not_knife_2025-11-27.md)
利 (lì) = 禾 (hé, grain) + 刀 (dāo, blade). Why this is a scythe operation, not knife-cutting. The π-sweep that harvests swaths where linear force exhausts. How 刀 (dāo) encodes complete tool geometry: curved blade ⊥ two-handed handle.

## Technology Encodings

### [The Loom Axiom](./meta/loom_axiom_identity_2025-11-27.md)
經 (jīng) as "warp threads," not "classic." The loom's geometry: warp tensioned first, weft passing through at perpendicular. How textile production encodes the same structure as the DDJ's 有/無 (yǒu/wú) relationship. The weaver's hands knew the math before anyone derived equations.

### [Forge Transformation Grammar](./meta/forge_transformation_grammar_2025-12-07.md)
剛 (gāng, hard) and 柔 (róu, soft) as properties distributed by cooling rate. The Wu-Yue bimetallic bronze sword as Chapter 76 written in metal. Why you can't force the 剛/柔 boundary—you create conditions and let physics work.

### [Radical Families: Grain and Blade](./meta/radical_families_grain_blade_2025-12-07.md)
禾 (hé) as substrate, 刂 (dāo) as operator. The transformation grammar: f(substrate, operator) → result. How the character system predicts which combinations make sense—and which don't exist.
